■ 5. INBOUND AI CALLING
Our Inbound AI Voice Agents answer every incoming call instantly — no hold music, no missed calls.
Powered by real-time speech-to-text, large language models, and text-to-speech, these agents sound
natural, understand context, and complete complex tasks autonomously.

How It Works
Step 1 — Call Received: Customer calls your business number (Twilio, Vonage, or existing PBX).
Step 2 — Speech-to-Text: Audio is streamed in real time to Deepgram or Whisper for instant transcription.
Step 3 — AI Processing: LLM processes the intent, queries knowledge base if needed, formulates response.
Step 4 — Text-to-Speech: ElevenLabs or Cartesia converts response to natural-sounding voice in under 500ms latency.
Step 5 — Action Taken: Bot books appointment, updates CRM, sends SMS, or transfers to human agent.
Step 6 — Call Summary: Full transcript and summary logged to CRM/Slack/email automatically.

Inbound Use Cases
- Dental/medical clinic appointment booking (24/7, no receptionist needed)
- Real estate inquiry handling and viewing scheduling
- Restaurant reservation management
- E-commerce order status and returns
- Legal firm first-intake qualification
- IT helpdesk Tier-1 support and ticket creation
- Hotel front desk — room info, check-in times, local recommendations

■ 10. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Will the AI sound robotic on calls?
A: No. Modern TTS engines like ElevenLabs produce voices that are virtually indistinguishable from
humans. We offer voice cloning if you want the agent to sound like a specific person on your team.

Q: Can the chatbot speak multiple languages?
A: Yes. Our WhatsApp and web chatbots support 50+ languages including Arabic, Urdu, Hindi, Spanish,
French, and German. The AI detects the customer's language automatically.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. For maximum privacy, we offer on-premise
deployments where no data leaves your own servers.

Q: What if the AI can't answer a question?
A: We configure fallback behaviours: the bot says it will check and follow up, sends an alert to a human,
or transfers the call/chat to a live agent — whichever fits your workflow.

Q: How do I update the chatbot's knowledge?
A: For RAG-based bots, simply update your source documents (Google Drive, Notion, website, PDF).
The system re-indexes automatically. For scripted flows, we provide a simple edit interface.

Q: Do you offer white-label solutions?
A: Yes. We offer a reseller / white-label programme for agencies who want to sell our AI solutions under
their own brand.

Q: How long does it take to go live?
A: A standard WhatsApp bot or n8n workflow takes 1–2 weeks. Voice AI agents typically take 2–4
weeks. Full agency stacks 4–6 weeks.

Q: What is n8n and why not use Zapier or Make?
A: n8n is open-source and self-hostable, meaning no per-task pricing and full data control. It also has
native AI nodes that Make and Zapier lack. We use all three platforms and recommend n8n for most
AI-heavy projects.

Q: Can the AI handle payment processing?
A: Yes. We integrate with Stripe, PayPal, and regional payment gateways. The AI can send payment
links, confirm transactions, and trigger fulfilment workflows.

Q: What happens after the support period?
A: You can extend with a monthly retainer or move to a self-managed model with full documentation we
provide. We also offer pay-as-you-go support tickets.
